RoyalMac International

The RoyalMac is a multinational business conglomerate with a core business segment of large volume import and export, general trading, and merchandising across the world, which was formed as a consortium with a strategic joint venture mentality. RoyalMac is a certified global partner in commodities and FMCG importing and exporting and operates multiple business venture sectors with its own strategic design tycoon globally with inventive thinking in technology and a professional workforce.
